Unicode normalization vulnerabilities

What is Unicode? Unicode or formally Unicode Standard is ab information technology standard for the consistent encoding, representation, and handling of text expressed in most of the world’s writing systems. Representation For example “A” is mapped to U+0041 and “a” is mapped to U+0061. Unicode characters exist from U+000000 to U+10FFFF (there are more than a million symbols).
